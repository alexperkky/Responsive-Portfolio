About Me

Hi there! My name is Alex Perkinson. I am beginning to learn web design, which I enjoy greatly. Before deciding to learn web design, I have worked as a pool professional, as well as a freight handler for McDonald's. Besides this, I love to play my guitar, play video games, watch pro wrestling, and hang out with my cats Buster Posey and Paul since I don't really have friends.

I have many goals in life, generally centered around creative endeavors. I hope to one day publish a book, improve my drawing skills from stick figures to something even slightly better, and to release some of the musical material I have had circling around my brain for years.
